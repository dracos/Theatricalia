Criticism

1. The action of criticizing, or passing judgement upon the qualities or merits of anything
2. The art of estimating the qualities and character of literary or artistic work;
   the function or work of a critic.

This site is run as a hobby; constructive positive criticism or other contact is welcome – especially if you’re from a theatre with a large archive you want to include on the site! :-)

Please note that all the information I have is on the site – I don’t have any further information on a particular production or person that I could give you. If you have an observation about a production, or a photograph concerning something on the site, please do add it to the site directly, thank you.
